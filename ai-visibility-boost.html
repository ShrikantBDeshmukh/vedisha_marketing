Why is your business invisible to AI?

The Problem

Traditional SEO focuses on links. But AI bots (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity) focus on entities and relationships. If your data isn't structured, you don't exist in their world.

The Prescription

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO). We move beyond keywords to build a semantic knowledge graph of your brand that AI assistants can trust and cite.
