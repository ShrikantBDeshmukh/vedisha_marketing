Our Story

After 8+ years leading marketing for brands across SaaS, D2C, and professional services — managing crores in ad spend, shipping hundreds of campaigns, and building content systems from scratch — we launched Vedisha Marketing to bring senior-level strategy to growth-stage companies without the overhead of a large agency. Every engagement is led by practitioners, not account managers.

Our Mission

Help ambitious teams ship better marketing: sharper positioning, repeatable content systems, reputation readiness, and experiments that create measurable business outcomes — not vanity metrics.

Our Process

Step 1

Discovery

We gather context quickly: your goals, audience, current assets, competitors, and what “success” looks like in plain numbers.

Step 2

Strategy & Messaging

We create a clear positioning direction, proof points, and a message hierarchy so every channel tells the same story.

Step 3

Build & Ship

We produce assets with a simple QA checklist, then ship in small batches so you get value quickly and we learn faster.

Step 4

Learn & Iterate

We review results, document what worked, and decide what to scale next. No dashboards for the sake of dashboards.
