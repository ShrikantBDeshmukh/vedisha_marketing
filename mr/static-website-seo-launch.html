FAQ

What is an "SEO Launch"?

It includes meta optimization, clean heading structure, sitemap generation, robots.txt, and setup for Google Search Console and Analytics.

Why choose static over WordPress?

Speed and security. Static sites load instantly, have no database to hack, and require near-zero maintenance.

What do I need to provide?

Content for your pages (or a brief we can expand), your logo, and any preferred images. We handle the rest.
