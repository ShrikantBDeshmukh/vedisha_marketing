AI bidding strategies playbook (April 2026)

In 2026, most manual bidding is replaced by platform AI. Your advantage is feeding the right data and setting a human strategy for creatives, local targeting and offers.

Google Ads (AI-first)

  • Target CPA for lead goals (clinics/coaching).
  • Target ROAS for revenue-focused businesses.
  • Maximize Conversions to build volume with budget caps.
  • PMax with strong audience signals and assets.
  • AI Max for Search as a scaling layer for broad queries.

Meta Ads (Advantage+)

  • Advantage+ Bidding (automated bidding across signals).
  • Value-based optimisation using customer value (where tracking supports it).
  • Broad targeting with strong creatives beats narrow interest stacking.
  • Cost/bid caps for controlled scaling in competitive niches.

What you do (human strategy)

  • Creative testing: offers, hooks, and local copy variations.
  • Conversion tracking readiness (pixels/events + CRM alignment).
  • Weekly decisions: what to scale, pause, or replace.
  • Landing page suggestions aligned to the ad’s promise.
