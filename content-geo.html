FAQ

Does GEO replace SEO?

No. GEO complements SEO. The same helpful content can perform in Google Search and also be easier for AI engines to cite and summarize.

What’s “AI citation building”?

In practice, it means publishing content that’s structured, credible, and internally consistent—plus strengthening your topical authority so AI systems have reasons to reference you.

How soon will we see results?

Content is cumulative. Many sites start seeing early movement in 6–12 weeks, with stronger compounding results over 3–6 months depending on competition and consistency.
