FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions. Search below, or email us if you don’t see your question.

What does Vedisha Marketing do?

We help brands grow through positioning, campaigns, content, performance marketing, PR, and reputation management—using AI to accelerate research and testing, and senior humans to shape strategy and creative quality.

How do you use AI without making our brand voice generic?

AI supports data analysis, variations, and operational speed. The core narrative, messaging hierarchy, and final creative choices are crafted and reviewed by senior strategists and creatives so output stays authentic and differentiated.

What timelines should we expect?

Timelines vary by scope. A positioning sprint can take 1–2 weeks, a campaign launch can take 2–6 weeks, and ongoing performance/PR programs run monthly. We’ll share an execution plan after an initial brief.

Do you work with startups and enterprises?

Yes. We work with early-stage teams that need clarity and speed, and with established brands that need scale, governance, and reliable execution.

How do we get started?

Email hello@vedishamarketing.com with your goals, audience, and timeline. We’ll reply with recommended next steps and a plan.
