The Positioning Kit

Move from vague to unignorable in 14 days.

"Positioning" often feels like an expensive, abstract exercise in philosophy. You pay an agency, you have three workshops, and you end up with a 50-page PDF that everyone ignores after a week. At Vedisha Marketing, we hate fluff as much as you do. We believe positioning is only valuable if it’s usable. That’s why we’ve compressed the process into a 2-week "Positioning Sprint" designed to move you from "vague and generic" to "sharp and unignorable."

Why "Positioning" is a Practical Growth Lever

Startups with a clearly defined "Primary Combatant" in their positioning see a 25% faster sales cycle. When you know exactly who you are for (and who you are NOT for), every decision—from ad copy to product roadmap—becomes easier.

Week 1: Deconstruction

We audit your competitors, category language, and customer objections. We identify the "White Space" where you can actually win.

Week 2: Construction

We build the narrative, proof points, and messaging hierarchy. We test the new "Way of Speaking" against real-world friction.
