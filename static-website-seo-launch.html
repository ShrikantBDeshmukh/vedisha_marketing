What moves the price?

Scope and polish—not buzzwords—drive the invoice.

Pages & design

  • Page count and custom layout depth
  • Photography vs stock assets
  • Motion and interaction complexity

Build approach

  • Template-based vs bespoke HTML/CSS
  • AI-assisted coding (still rare in agency copy) vs fully manual

SEO & hosting

  • Basic launch vs technical SEO audit
  • Domain + first-year hosting (often ₹3k–₹7k extra if itemised)

FAQ

What does “SEO launch” mean here?

Usually: meta titles/descriptions, clean headings, sitemap/robots, Search Console and Analytics setup, performance basics, and help with first indexing. Ongoing SEO is a separate conversation.

Do you only work in Pune and Aurangabad?

We publish Maharashtra-focused pricing because that’s where most of our enquiries land—but static builds are remote-friendly. Email us your scope.

Can you match my industry (restaurant, clinic, retail)?

Yes. Share your segment, locations, and any design references; we’ll align copy and layout without sounding generic.

What do you need to start?

Logo/branding, page text (or bullet points we can expand), photos or stock direction, domain/hosting preference, and one competitor site you like (or dislike).
