# Vedisha Marketing: Career Growth Ladder & Compensation Structure

This document outlines the professional progression paths at Vedisha Marketing, distinguishing between skill-building roles (stipend-based) and professional execution roles (salaried).

---

## Stage 1: Intern (Learning Phase)
**Nature:** Learning-first, task-oriented.  
**Compensation:** **Fee-Based Training.** (Student pays for AI mastery and certification).  
**Focus:** Skill acquisition, AI tool mastery, and agency culture immersion.

*   **Primary Goal:** Master the "Vedisha Way" of AI orchestration.
*   **Expectation:** 100% focus on learning and shadowing senior leads.
*   **Outcome:** Certificate of Completion + Real-world Portfolio.

---

## Stage 2: Trainee (Industrial Immersion)
**Nature:** Practical execution, project-oriented.  
**Compensation:** **Self-Funded Industrial Training.** (Includes live project access and portfolio building).  
**Focus:** Applying skills to live client projects under direct supervision.

*   **Primary Goal:** Deliver measurable results for assigned client tasks (e.g., SEO clusters, Ad variations).
*   **Expectation:** Proactive participation in team sprints and basic client reporting.
*   **Outcome:** Industrial Training Certificate + Letter of Recommendation.

---

## Stage 3: Executive (Professional Execution)
**Nature:** Full ownership of day-to-day operations.  
**Compensation:** **Salaried Job** (Fixed Salary + Performance Bonus).  
**Focus:** Independent delivery and maintaining high-quality standards.

*   **Primary Goal:** Manage client accounts or specific service lines (e.g., SEO department) with minimal oversight.
*   **Expectation:** Consistent delivery of KPIs (ROAS, Ranking, Lead Quality).
*   **Outcome:** Full-time employment benefits + Professional growth opportunities.

---

## Stage 4: Specialist (Strategic Ownership)
**Nature:** Strategic leadership and advanced technical mastery.  
**Compensation:** **Senior Salaried Job** (High Fixed Salary + Profit Sharing/Bonus).  
**Focus:** Large-scale strategy, innovation, and mentoring juniors.

*   **Primary Goal:** Architect complex growth systems and drive agency-level innovation.
*   **Expectation:** Ownership of major client outcomes and department-level efficiency.
*   **Outcome:** Leadership role + Direct contribution to the agency's growth roadmap.

---

## Progression Timelines (General Guide)
1.  **Intern → Trainee:** 3 Months (Based on skill assessment).
2.  **Trainee → Executive:** 3–6 Months (Based on performance in industrial projects).
3.  **Executive → Specialist:** 12–18 Months (Based on consistent result delivery and strategic depth).

---

## Summary of Distinction
| Feature | Intern / Trainee | Executive / Specialist |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Focus** | Skill Acquisition & Learning | Result Delivery & Ownership |
| **Financial Model** | **Training Fee** (Student Pays) | **Salary** (Company Pays) |
| **Responsibility** | Assigned Tasks | Account/Strategic Ownership |
| **Mentorship** | High (Shadowing) | Strategic (Guidance) |

*Vedisha Marketing believes in "Learning by Doing." Every role is a paid opportunity, ensuring our team is motivated to build the future of marketing.*
