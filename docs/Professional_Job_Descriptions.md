# AI-Driven Professional Roles: Executive & Specialist Descriptions
**Company:** Vedisha Marketing  
**Role Type:** Salaried (Fixed Salary + Performance Incentives)  
**Location:** Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, Maharashtra  

This document outlines the professional expectations for our salaried roles. These positions require independent execution, strategic thinking, and a track record of delivering measurable ROI.

---

## 1. AI Marketing Strategist (Executive / Specialist)
**Role Overview:**  
The AI Marketing Strategist is responsible for architecting and managing the high-level growth engines for our clients. You translate complex business problems into automated marketing funnels that convert.

**Key Responsibilities:**  
*   Designing end-to-end customer journeys using AI-driven predictive modeling.
*   Leading "Positioning Sprints" for new clients to define their unique market voice.
*   Managing a team of Interns/Trainees to ensure strategy is executed with precision.
*   Directly overseeing high-budget accounts and ensuring client ROI targets are met.
*   Building custom GPTs and internal agency workflows to reduce operational friction.

**Success Metrics:**  
*   Total Revenue/Lead Growth for assigned accounts.
*   Client retention rate and upsell success.
*   Innovation in agency-wide strategy templates.

---

## 2. Performance Marketing Executive
**Role Overview:**  
A results-obsessed role focused on managing paid media spend across Meta, Google, and LinkedIn. You are responsible for every rupee spent and every lead generated.

**Key Responsibilities:**  
*   Managing monthly ad spends of ₹5L – ₹50L+ with strict ROAS/CPA targets.
*   Setting up advanced tracking (Server-side GTM, Offline Conversions).
*   Implementing AI-driven bidding strategies and custom audience segmentation.
*   Drafting and testing 50+ ad creative variations per month using DCO.
*   Weekly reporting and strategic pivot recommendations for clients.

**Success Metrics:**  
*   Achieving or exceeding target ROAS/CPL.
*   Ad creative performance scores (CTR/Hook Rate).

---

## 3. AI SEO & AEO Specialist
**Role Overview:**  
You own the "Organic Growth" department. Your goal is to make sure our clients are the #1 answer everywhere—from Google Search to Perplexity and ChatGPT.

**Key Responsibilities:**  
*   Managing full-site SEO migrations and complex technical audits.
*   Developing and executing "Semantic Content Hub" strategies.
*   Implementing advanced Schema.org architectures for AI search assistant visibility.
*   Monitoring "AI Citations" and ensuring brand authority in generative engines.
*   Leading the SEO content team to produce 50+ high-ranking assets monthly.

**Success Metrics:**  
*   Organic traffic growth and "Position Zero" rankings.
*   Growth in "AI Assistant" mentions for client brands.

---

## 4. AI Marketing Automation Architect (Specialist)
**Role Overview:**  
A technical leadership role focused on building the agency's and clients' infrastructure. You are a no-code/low-code wizard who connects everything to everything.

**Key Responsibilities:**  
*   Building enterprise-level n8n/Make workflows for lead nurturing and CRM sync.
*   Architecting custom AI Chatbot systems with RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation).
*   Integrating AI tools into client's existing ERP/CRM systems (Salesforce, HubSpot, etc.).
*   Developing internal agency tools to automate reporting and QA.
*   Ensuring 99.9% uptime for all automated marketing pipelines.

**Success Metrics:**  
*   Operational hours saved through automation.
*   System reliability and integration success rate.

---

## 5. Creative Lead – AI Content & Design (Specialist)
**Role Overview:**  
You bridge the gap between "Machine Output" and "Human Taste." You ensure every asset shipped is world-class, on-brand, and emotionally resonant.

**Key Responsibilities:**  
*   Setting the creative direction for multi-channel campaigns.
*   Managing the agency's visual language and "Prompt Engineering" standards.
*   Overseeing the production of high-fidelity AI-generated video and image assets.
*   Ensuring brand voice consistency across 10+ different client niches.
*   Mentoring junior creators on the art of "Humanizing" AI drafts.

**Success Metrics:**  
*   Creative performance (Conversion rate of assets).
*   Client satisfaction with brand aesthetics and narrative quality.
