# AI-Driven Internship & Trainee Programs: Position Descriptions
**Company:** Vedisha Marketing  
**Company Type:** Modern Digital Marketing Agency  
**Location:** Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, Maharashtra (On-site / Hybrid)  
**Duration:** 3 Months  
**Internship Type:** Stipend-based (Performance Incentives Included)  
**Target Candidates:** Recent Graduates, Final-year Students, and Career Changers

## 1. Trainee AI Marketing Strategist
**Role Summary:**  
The Trainee AI Marketing Strategist supports the senior strategy team in architecting multi-channel growth systems. You will assist in translating client business goals into AI-enhanced marketing funnels, ensuring a seamless blend of human creativity and machine efficiency.

**Key Learning Objectives:**  
*   Mastering "Chain-of-Thought" prompting for complex strategy development.
*   Understanding the "Human-in-the-Loop" (HITL) agency workflow.
*   Developing predictive buyer personas using advanced LLM analysis.

**Primary Responsibilities:**  
*   Conducting AI-powered market research and competitor gap analysis using Perplexity Pro.
*   Drafting 12-month strategic roadmaps using Claude 3.5 Sonnet based on client briefs.
*   Brainstorming campaign "hooks" and unique selling propositions (USPs) with generative tools.
*   Assisting in the creation of brand positioning kits and message hierarchies.
*   Mapping out automated customer journeys in collaboration with the automation team.
*   Transcribing and summarizing client strategy sessions using AI meeting assistants.

**Required Qualifications:**  
*   Basic understanding of marketing funnel concepts (AIDA model).
*   Enthusiasm for LLMs (ChatGPT, Claude) and prompt engineering.
*   Strong analytical thinking and written communication skills.

**Mentorship & Reporting:**  
Reports directly to the Founder & Strategy Lead. Weekly 1-on-1 strategy reviews and feedback sessions.

**Success Metrics:**  
*   Accuracy and depth of AI-generated market research reports.
*   Number of strategic "hooks" adopted for live client campaigns.
*   Completion of the internal "AI Strategic Thinking" certification.

**Potential Full-Time Conversion Path:**  
Top performers will be considered for a **Junior AI Marketing Strategist** role.

**Mentorship & Reporting:**

## 3. Intern – AI SEO & GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)
**Role Summary:**  
The AI SEO Intern assists in modern search optimization tasks, focusing on ranking in both traditional search engines and AI assistants. You will help implement the "AEO" (Answer Engine Optimization) playbook to ensure clients are cited by LLMs.

**Key Learning Objectives:**  
*   Understanding the shift from Keyword SEO to Semantic/Generative SEO.
*   Learning to implement LLM-friendly schema markup.
*   Mastering AI-driven technical audits and site health monitoring.

**Primary Responsibilities:**  
*   Performing GEO audits using Perplexity to see how clients are cited by AI models.
*   Generating semantic keyword clusters using AI analysis of search intent.
*   Drafting "Answer Engine" FAQs and structured data snippets for web pages.
*   Identifying content gaps by comparing client sites with competitors via AI scraping.
*   Optimizing meta-tags and headers at scale using programmatic AI tools.
*   Monitoring site speed and technical health using AI-integrated audit tools.

**Required Qualifications:**  
*   Basic understanding of how Google Search works.
*   Familiarity with Google Search Console or Google Analytics.
*   Highly organized with strong attention to technical detail.

**Mentorship & Reporting:**  
Reports to the SEO Specialist. Weekly technical deep-dives and ranking analysis meetings.

**Success Metrics:**  
*   Number of "Position Zero" or AI citation wins for assigned clients.
*   Increase in organic health scores across audit dashboards.
*   Successful deployment of 5+ schema-rich content clusters.

**Potential Full-Time Conversion Path:**  
Eligible for conversion to **Junior AI SEO/AEO Specialist**.
